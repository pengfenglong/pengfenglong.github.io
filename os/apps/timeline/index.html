Welcome to a demo timeline of our new Category Band features.

We have just released some new improvements to Category Band timelines that we hope you will like. Category Band timelines (which can be activated from the Setting's tab - see the View Type option) split the timeline into vertical bands for each category that you create.;xNLx;;xNLx;Stories you have put in a category will appear in the related category band. This in effect allows you to create multiple timelines in one. Although users love our unique Category Band feature, it has not been as customisable as we would have liked. For a start, the bands were ordered alphabetically by the category name, and nothing you could do could change that ordering. Until now, that is. We have now provided a new 'Order' field for each category (see the Categories tab in the grey admin panel). The categories will now be ordered by this number, in ascending order. The lower this value, the earlier in the sequence the category's band will appear.;xNLx;;xNLx;In addition, we have also provided a new 'Size' field. This allows you to manipulate the size of a particular band, allowing you to have bands of varying size, just like with this timeline. We hope you like this new feature, and look forward to seeing any fancy Category Band timelines you have created. Why not tell us about them by emailing us at hello@tiki-toki.com.
